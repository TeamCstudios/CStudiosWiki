CStudios Survival

Background

CStudios Survival was started on November 16, 2013 by MrJoCrafter as the first series on the Team CStudios, then cstudiosyoutube, channel.
CStudios Survival ran for 3 seasons between 2013 and 2016 with several hiatuses in between each. It is currently discontinued, with the last episode being Season 3, Episode 12 on November 13, 2016.

Season 1

Season 1 had 6 episodes and started in 2013.
Season 1 was less of a "survival" series, and more in line with a roleplay series, with things happening that wouldn't happen in a normal survival game.
This season consisted of MrJoCrafter collecting resources while fighting a group of evil (maybe) sheep.

Season 2

Season 2 had 5 episodes and started in 2016.
Season 2 was more of a survival series, but still had some roleplay aspects.
This season continued the plot of Season 1.

Season 3

Season 3 had 12 episodes, also started in 2016 and was the last season.
This season was a full survival series, with an entirely new world.

Plot

MrJoCrafter first built a house, then found some strange sheep.
Then, after some exploring, he found a nation full of strangely colored sheep.
After the sheep tried to blow up his house, a tower appeared on a nearby island.
MrJoCrafter conquered the tower, killed the king of the sheep, and burned the sheep nation.
